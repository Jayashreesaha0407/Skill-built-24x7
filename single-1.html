Course Overview

This comprehensive 20-day course is designed to equip participants with the skills required to master Google Earth Engine (GEE). From basic operations to advanced geospatial analysis, participants will gain hands-on experience in remote sensing, land use change detection, vegetation indices, and machine learning applications. The course covers vector and raster data processing, GIS-based spatial analysis, time-series trend analysis, supervised and unsupervised classification, and multiple index calculations. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced user, this course will provide step-by-step guidance on using GEE for real-world applications.

Course Outcomes
